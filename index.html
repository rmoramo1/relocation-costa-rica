Covid-19 has left most nations devastated with collapsed economies which will be witness to long lasting ripples of the pandemic for years to come. Costa Rica, a gem in the rough, was able to handle the pandemic with relative ease, full lockdowns were scarce, minor curfews and restrictions set in place. To date, CR only had less than 2900 deaths due to covid-19 and thanks to Costa Rica’s amazing public and private health care over 190,000 people have recovered from the virus

Now the citizens of CR have reopened their businesses to operate at near full capacity and both air and land borders are welcoming tourists once again. During the pandemic national unemployment skyrocketed to 24% but has rapidly declined to 19% since reopening the economy in January 2021 and the trend will continue as foriegn interest has spiked and tourism becomes attractive with easy to obtain entry requirements.

A variety of new multi billion dollar projects have been announced to begin development in 2021:

  • Metropolitan International Airport (Orotina) which includes a ‘mini city’
  • Electric passenger train
  • Discovery Costa Rica, the theme park located in Guanacaste
  • Dry Canal

Due to the recent pandemic, sellers have become extremely flexible and property prices are at a once in a lifetime low, creating a more than attractive buyers marke

RCR can help you take advantage of the best investment opportunities. By conducting careful analytic studies we can present and predict which investments will have the best Return on Investment (ROI) in the near future.

Costa Rica offers the ultimate living accommodations for new arrivals. Costa Ricans are warm loving, well educated individuals who are often bilingual. Eager to work at affordable wages, Costa Rica’s workforce is the most sophisticated in central america which makes for a smooth and easy transition to open and operate businesses internationally. RCR can even go the extra mile to accompany you through your headhunting and hiring process.
